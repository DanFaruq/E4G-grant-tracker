✨ Enhancements added on top of the base design

🎯
KPI cards are clickable filters — tap Overdue to instantly filter the list below. Tap again to clear.
↕️
Inline status toggle — click the status badge on any task to change it (To do → In progress → Done) without opening the task.
🎨
Urgency left-border coloring — overdue = red, due today = orange, in progress = blue. Visible at a glance.
👥
"With" avatars — shows other people on multi-assignee tasks so you know who else is working on it.
📁
Clickable grant pills — each task/event shows its linked grant as a pill that navigates directly to the grant page.
👁
Admin "View as" dropdown — admins can switch to any team member's My Work page for a manager view.
📅
Date badges on events — calendar-style day/month boxes. Recurrence shown with a ↻ Weekly tag.
🗂
Grant cards at the bottom — compact cards for every grant you're assigned to, with stage pill and deadline indicator.
