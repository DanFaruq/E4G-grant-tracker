Why Option C wins — UX rationale

  • Equal visual weight for all three pillars — you asked to "accommodate the two new features," not tuck them away. Options A and B both subordinate them.
  • Immediate situational awareness — colour-coded stat cards (amber / indigo / green) let a user assess the platform state in under 2 seconds.
  • Clear information hierarchy — top row = overview, bottom row = urgency. Follows the UX principle: show what's happening, then show what needs action.
  • Scales to mobile — three cards stack vertically on small screens, no layout surgery needed.
  • Activity feed on dashboard — shows the team is alive and working; critical for a team management tool, not just a grant tracker.
