Common Questions
How long does a project take? +
Landing pages and simple sites: 3–7 days. Internal tools and web apps: 3–6 weeks. Complex platforms: 8–12 weeks. I give you a fixed timeline after our first call.
Do you do design too, or just development? +
Both. I design the interface and build it. You don't need to hire a separate designer — I work from your brief, a rough sketch, or from scratch. For larger projects I use Figma to align before building.
What do you need from me to get started? +
A description of the problem you want solved, who uses it, and any existing tools or data to work with. I handle everything else — design, architecture, build, and deployment.
Do you do ongoing maintenance? +
Yes. After the 2-week post-launch support period I offer monthly retainers for ongoing development and maintenance. Rates depend on scope.
I'm not technical — will I understand what's being built? +
Absolutely. I write in plain English, send weekly Loom updates, and give you a live preview link from day one. You stay in the loop without needing to understand code.
