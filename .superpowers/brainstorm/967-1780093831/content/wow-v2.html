Common Questions
How long does a project take? +
Most projects take 3–6 weeks from kickoff to launch. Simple internal tools can be done in 2 weeks. Complex platforms with multiple integrations may take 8–10 weeks. I'll give you an accurate timeline after our first call.
What do you need from me to get started? +
A clear description of the problem you're solving, who uses the app, and any existing tools or data we need to work with. I handle the rest — design, architecture, build, and deployment.
Do you do ongoing maintenance? +
Yes. After the 2-week post-launch support period I offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing feature development and maintenance. Rates depend on scope.
What if I'm not technical — will I understand what's being built? +
Absolutely. I write in plain English, send weekly video updates via Loom, and give you a live preview link from day one. You don't need to know code to stay in the loop.
